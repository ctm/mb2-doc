Overview

Everything here is subject to change until it is finalized, probably in early May. Until then, craftpoker.com may even choose to not run this series. That said, a lot of work is going into making this happen.

WSOP-Style 2024 should be fairly similar to WSOP-Style 2023, although there will be no option to receive a physical prize, only cash via Venmo or Zelle. With luck, up to five places will pay as was done for WYWAE 2024.
